Rooted in Kolhapur

Welcome to Nishad Enterprises, a passionate small business hailing from the vibrant heart of Kolhapur. For generations, Kolhapuri food has been synonymous with bold flavors, heat, and an unforgettable aroma.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to preserve and share the authentic, homemade taste of real Kolhapuri masala with the world. We meticulously hand-select our spices to ensure every spoonful carries the rich heritage and fiery soul of our hometown.
